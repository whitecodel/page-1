E-Cell@RCMB Bangalore
The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at the Regional College of Management Bangalore is functioning in the college campus with the intention of nurturing entrepreneurship skills of the students.

Its a student-driven initiative along with the experienced faculty as the mentors aiming to help in nurturing business ideas, building network with target consumers, investors & bankers to start and manage startups.

RCMB E-Cell provides practical experience in the field of entrepreneurship to its students on campus through workshops, seminars, simulations, case studies, Entrepreneur Talk Series, field visits, industry visits, entrepreneurship clubs.

Objectives of RCMB E-Cell

The main objective of this E-cell is to create entrepreneurs and future business leaders through practical approach
