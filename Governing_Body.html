Governing Body
Being the apex body of the institute, the Governing Body consists of eminent educationists, social workers, senior academicians, public personalities, industry experts, and representatives from UGC, Affiliating University and the State Government. The governing Body takes strategic decision s and provides overall directions for development and growth of the institution. The policy decisions taken by this apex body enables the institute in achieving its goal as enshrined in the institutes vision and mission
