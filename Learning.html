Library
RCM has well equipped library in both the campuses. The fully automated library has over 33,000 volumes, 150 National and International subscriptions journals, online database of articles and journals, company annual reports, back volume journals and over two dozen daily newspapers. The library at Bhubaneshwar has been approved for Doctoral and Post-Doctoral research by the UGC.
